How to Play

In this fun game, you control Spongebob as he tries to avoid being caught by Doodlebob. Move Spongebob around the screen using your mouse to dodge Doodlebob.

If Spongebob gets too close to Doodlebob, you'll lose the game! Try to keep Spongebob away for as long as possible.

Click the "Reset" button at any time to start a new game and try to improve your score.
